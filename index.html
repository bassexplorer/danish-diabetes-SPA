Gulyás soup with root herbs

45

4

Energy 1175 kJ

Carbohydrate 20 g

Fat 11 g

Ingredients:

150 g of lean beef in strips or cubes
1 tsp. fat
1 onion (about 100 g)
1 clove of garlic
½ ds. peeled tomatoes, preferably chopped
½ l beef broth
1 bay leaf
1 tsp. mild pepper
1 tbsp. encountered strong chilli
salt, pepper
1 small parsnip (about 100 g)
¼ small celery (about 75 g)
2 carrots (about 150 g)
Garnish
Possibly. chopped parsley

Instructions:

Sweat the beef into the fat. Arrow and chop onions and garlic. Sweat the onions for a few minutes. Add peeled tomatoes, broth, bay leaves and spices. Cook the soup for approx. 20 min. Peel parsley, celery and carrots and cut into cubes or slices. Add the vegetables and allow the soup to cook further for approx. 10 min. Taste the soup for. Serving If : necessary gold ash soup with root herbs with chopped parsley and serve with 1 slice (40 g) bread per person.

Tomato soup

35

1

Energy 1450 kJ

Carbohydrate 25 g

Fat 14 g

Ingredients:

½ leeks (about 60 g)
1 small carrot (about 50 g)
1 slice of cabbage (about 200 g)
75 g minced beef with a maximum of 10% fat
1 tsp. liquid fat
2½ dl vegetable broth
½ ds. peeled tomatoes (200 g)
½ tbsp. tomato paste, preferably contentrate
½ tsp. mild pepper
¼ tsp. dried basil
salt, pepper

Instructions:

Cut the leek into thin rings. Cut the carrot into small cubes. Cut the cabbage finely. Brown the meat in the fat. Add the vegetables and sweat them. Add the remaining ingredients and cook the soup for 10-15 minutes. Taste the soup for. Serve tomato soup with whole grain bread. You can replace the carrot or some of the cabbage with celery.

Moussaka

60

6

Energy 1750 kJ

Carbohydrate 21 g

Fat 27 g

Ingredients:

2 eggplants (about 500 g)
1 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. oil
4 potatoes (about 300 g)
2 onions (about 200 g)
1-2 cloves of garlic to taste
400 g minced lamb with maximum
10-12% fat
1 ds. peeled tomatoes, preferably chopped (400 g)
1 small ds. contentrated tomato puree (70 g)
1 tsp. dried oregano
salt, pepper
2½ dl Greek yogurt 0.2% fat
2 eggs
50 g of grated cheese with a maximum of 17% fat
½ tsp. fine nutmeg
½ tsp. salt

Instructions:

Cut the eggplant into approx. ½ cm thick slices. Sprinkle with salt and allow to soak for at least ½ hour. Wipe the slices and fry them briefly in the oil several times. Take them off the pan. Cut the potatoes into thin slices and place in an ovenproof dish for approx. 30 x 22 cm. Chop the onion and garlic and sweat it in the rest of the oil. Add the meat and fry it for a few mins. Add peeled tomatoes, tomato puree and spices and cook for about 10 minutes. without a lid so some of the moisture evaporates. Lay eggplant slices and meat mixture layered in the dish on top of the potato slices. Whisk yogurt, eggs and cheese with the spices and pour the mixture over the dish. Bake at 200 ° C for approx. 45 min. on middle oven rack. Cover stanniol if dish becomes too brown or move dish into oven. Server Moussaka with whole grain bread and salad for. You can use several potatoes instead of one eggplant. Squash can replace eggplant. Minced beef can replace lamb. 3 tbsp. grated parmesan cheese can be mixed in the yogurt instead of grated cheese.

Kale salad with rye kernels

25

4

Energy 1625 kJ

Carbohydrate 44 g

Fat 16 g

Ingredients:

150 g rye kernels or whole grain rice
150 g of fresh kale leaves
50 g of edamame beans
1 apple (about 100 g)
1 red onion (about 100 g)
1 tsp. oil
½ tsp. smoked paprika
1 pinch of salt
50 g almonds
50 g salad with a maximum of 17% fat
1½ tbsp. oil
4 tbsp. apple vinegar
2 tsp. honey
salt, pepper

Instructions:

Boil rye kernels or whole grain rice as directed on the packaging and cool them. Remove the stick from the kale and chop the cabbage very finely. Pour boiling water over the beans and cool them. Cut the apple into thin slices. Halve the onion and cut it into thin strips. Heat the oil - do not smoke - and add the peppers, salt and almonds. Shake them for a few minutes, so the paprika has spread to the almonds. Cool them down. Mix all ingredients for the marinade. Mix rice or grains together with vegetables and salad and turn in marinade. the salatterns before turning them in. Chop the almonds roughly and spread them over the salad just before serving. Serve the salad as a breakfast dish - you can possibly. add a leftover chicken. You can buy ready-to-use rye seeds. Pour if necessary boiling water over them first.

Chocolate rolls

60

20 pcs

Energy 625 kJ

Carbohydrate 26 g

Fat 2 g

Ingredients:

10 g of yeast
4 parts water
2 parts soured milk product with a maximum of 1.5% fat
1 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. honey, preferably liquid
1 tsp. vanilla sugar
100 g oatmeal, coarsely rolled (about 3 dl)
300 g rye flour (approx. 6 dl)
ca. 300 g wheat flour (about 6 dl)
75 g of dark chocolate with at least 70% cocoa

Instructions:

Stir the yeast in cold water. Add the other ingredients except chocolate and stir well together. It should be a little sticky. Chop the chocolate roughly and stir in. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and allow to rise 6-8 hours, preferably overnight in refrigerator. Stir in the dough and divide into 20 greased cupcake molds. You can also put the dough on a baking sheet with baking paper. Allow the buns to simmer for approx. 20 min. Bake the rye balls at 200 ° C for approx. 25 min. on middle oven rack. Cool the buns on a baking dish. Serve rye breads with chocolate as a snack. They can be eaten as they are. Rugs with chocolate can be frozen.
